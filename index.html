Centrally Located

Located within Gem Residences, Gem Fitness offers accessibility to facilities to over 1000 units.
At the heart of Toa Payoh, it is also less than a 10 minute walk away from most of the flats in Toa Payoh Central, and a 10 minute walk away from Braddell MRT Station.

A fitness centre for calisthenics or rehabilitative stretching


Enjoy a game with friends can have access to a tennis court
