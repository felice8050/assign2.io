Find the right class for your fitness needs!

Swimming is beneficial for both strength and cardiovascular health. In addition, learning to swim can help to build one's confidence in water and is potentially life-saving! Our instructors take a progressive approach, first teaching you confidence in water through breathing techniques, before going into mastery with the various styles of swimming.

Tennis is known as the "sport for a lifetime". A social game that improves cardiovascular health as well as agility, tennis courts are available all over Singapore and in most condominiums. Yet many find it a daunting sport to try out. Learn all about this sport, its rules and techniques with our certified coaches.

Yoga might seem like a boring and ineffective form of exercise, yet it is surprisingly effective in improving core strength, balance and flexibility! Beyond the physical benefits, yoga also improves one's energy, mood and helps with stress relief.
